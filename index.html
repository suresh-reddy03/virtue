Sree Urban Pinnacle

A project by Virtue Infrastructures


Designs that fieathe your class.Interiors which are a class apart Striking a perfect balance between utility function and aesthetics, SREE Urban Pinnacle is a proven testimony of the art of designing and planning spaces matching the mind-set of the occupants. When the whole architecture is conceived in a waythat reflects the trends, techniques and styles of moment, special care is taken to ensure that you live a peaceful life away from the concrete jungle. The tonality, themes and colour codes were chosen after enormous research on the behavior, way of thinking and over-all life-style of the residents. In short, at SREE Urban Pinnacle every square inch brings alive the vividness and dynamism which defines your living here.

specifications

STRUCTURE:RCC framed Structure WALLS
6" thick cement solid block for exterior walls & 4" thick cement solid block for interior walls

KITCHEN:
Granite slab of 20mm thick and stainless steel sink, glazed tiles dado upto 2ft., height above the platform.
WINDOWS:
3 Track fabrication windows for living and bedroom with safety grills and 2 Track for kitchen and bathroom with safety grills
DOORS:
Main Door with teak wood frame with OST shutters & all other doors with Hardwood door with flush door shutters

WATER SUPPLY:
24 hours water supply from Borewell with the Overhead tank and sump. Cauvery water provision in kitchen
POWER BACKUP:
Generator for common area, Lift & for each flat (Lighting points).

TV & Telephone:
Individual TV & Telephone points in Living and Master Bedroo.

ELECTRICAL:
Concealed copper wiring with Anchor/Roma switches and sockets.
